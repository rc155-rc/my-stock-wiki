# TSM Q1 FY2026 Earnings Report
**Date:** April 16, 2026
**Ticker:** [[TSM]]

## Executive Summary
TSMC reported a blowout Q1 2026 with $35.9 billion in revenue (+40.6% YoY) and record gross margins of 66.2%. The results confirm the massive pull-forward of 3nm and 2nm demand from AI and high-end smartphone customers.

## Financial Performance (Actuals)
| Metric | Q1 2026 (Act) | Q1 2025 (Act) | Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Revenue (USD)** | $35,900M | $25,530M | +40.6% |
| **Gross Margin %** | 66.2% | 53.1% | +1310bps |
| **Operating Margin %** | 58.1% | 42.0% | +1610bps |
| **EPS (NT$)** | $22.08 | $13.95 | +58.3% |

## Technology Node Revenue Mix
- **3-nanometer:** 25% of wafer revenue.
- **5-nanometer:** 36% of wafer revenue.
- **7-nanometer:** 13% of wafer revenue.
- **Advanced Technologies (7nm+):** 74% of total revenue.

## Guidance (Q2 FY2026)
- **Revenue:** $39.0B - $40.2B (Massive sequential growth).
- **Gross Margin:** 65.5% - 67.5%.
- **Operating Margin:** 56.5% - 58.5%.

## Ecosystem & News
- **HPC & AI:** Continued strong demand for AI accelerators from [[NVDA]] and custom silicon for [[AAPL]].
- **Margin Power:** The jump to 66% gross margin indicates unprecedented pricing power on 3nm/2nm nodes.
- **2nm Status:** Management indicated 2nm "N2" ramp is ahead of schedule for 2H 2026 mass production.
