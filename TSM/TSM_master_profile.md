# [[TSM]] - Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Co. Master Profile

## 1. Corporate Identity
**Business Overview:** The world's largest dedicated independent semiconductor foundry.
**Leadership:** C.C. Wei (CEO).
**The Moat:** Advanced process technology leadership (3nm, 2nm), massive scale, and deep integration with top-tier fabless companies.

## 2. Business Segments
- **High Performance Computing (HPC):** Data center, AI chips.
- **Smartphone:** Primarily Apple's A-series and M-series chips.
- **IoT, Automotive, Digital Consumer Electronics.**

## 3. The Ecosystem Graph
- **Suppliers:** [[ASML]] (EUV lithography), [[AMAT]], [[LRCX]].
- **Customers:** [[AAPL]], [[NVDA]], [[AMD]], [[AVGO]], [[QCOM]].
- **Competitors:** [[INTC]], [[SSNLF]].
