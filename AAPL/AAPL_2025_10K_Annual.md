# AAPL 2025 Annual Report (10-K)
**Date:** September 27, 2025 (Fiscal Year End)
**Ticker:** [[AAPL]]

## Executive Summary
Apple Inc. reported record net sales of $416.2 billion for fiscal 2025, a 6% increase over 2024. Net income reached $112.0 billion with a diluted EPS of $7.46.

## Financial Performance (Actuals)
| Metric | 2025 (Act) | 2024 (Act) | Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Net Sales** | $416,161M | $391,035M | +6% |
| **Net Income** | $112,010M | $93,736M | +19% |
| **Diluted EPS** | $7.46 | $6.08 | +23% |
| **Gross Margin %** | 46.9% | 46.2% | +70bps |

## Segment Net Sales
| Segment | 2025 Sales | 2024 Sales | Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **iPhone** | $209,586M | $201,183M | +4% |
| **Mac** | $33,708M | $29,984M | +12% |
| **iPad** | $28,023M | $26,694M | +5% |
| **Wearables, Home & Acc.** | $35,686M | $37,005M | -4% |
| **Services** | $109,158M | $96,169M | +14% |

## Geographic Net Sales
- **Americas:** $178,353M (+7%)
- **Europe:** $111,032M (+10%)
- **Greater China:** $64,377M (-4%)
- **Japan:** $28,703M (+15%)
- **Rest of Asia Pacific:** $33,696M (+10%)

## The Ecosystem & Supply Chain
- **Manufacturing:** Substantially all hardware is manufactured by outsourcing partners in China mainland, India, Japan, South Korea, Taiwan, and Vietnam.
- **Key Partners (Inferred/Known):** [[TSM]] (Foundries), [[NVDA]] (AI infrastructure for services), [[ASML]] (Lithography for silicon partners).
- **Concentration Risk:** Two vendors accounted for 46% and 23% of vendor non-trade receivables. One customer represented 10%+ of accounts receivable.

## Risk Factors
- Macroeconomic conditions and global trade tensions (especially regarding Greater China).
- Reliance on third-party intellectual property and manufacturing.
- Competitive pressure in the smartphone and services markets.
- Regulatory scrutiny regarding the App Store and Digital Markets Act (DMA).
