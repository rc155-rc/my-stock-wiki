# [[AAPL]] - Apple Inc. Master Profile

## 1. Corporate Identity
**Business Overview:** Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones, personal computers, tablets, wearables, and accessories, and sells a variety of related services.
**Leadership:** Tim Cook (CEO), Kevan Parekh (CFO - incoming/current as of late 2025).
**The Moat:** Vertical integration of hardware/software, massive installed base (2B+ active devices), high brand loyalty, and the "Services" ecosystem lock-in.

## 2. Business Segments
- **iPhone:** The primary revenue driver (50%+ of sales).
- **Services:** High-margin growth engine including App Store, iCloud, Apple Music, Apple Pay, etc.
- **Mac & iPad:** Personal and professional computing.
- **Wearables, Home and Accessories:** Includes Apple Watch, AirPods, and Vision Pro.

## 3. The Ecosystem Graph
- **Suppliers:** [[TSM]], [[NVDA]], [[ASML]], [[INTC]], [[LPL]], [[SWKS]], [[QRVO]], [[AVGO]], [[STM]].
- **Customers:** Global consumer market, Enterprise sector, Education.
- **Competitors:** [[MSFT]], [[GOOGL]], [[AMZN]], [[META]], [[NVDA]] (in AI/Silicon), [[SSNLF]] (Samsung).

## 6. AI Opinion & Synthesis
- **Stance:** Strong Bullish
- **Thesis:** Apple has shattered the "China slowdown" narrative with a 38% recovery in the region. The iPhone 17 is clearly a supercycle-level product, driving 23% growth. The pivot to Services (76.5% margin) and the new [[GOOGL]] partnership for AI foundation models positions Apple as the dominant consumer AI platform. Succession risk is minimized by the Ternus appointment. The only near-term headwind is supply-side constraints at [[TSM]].
