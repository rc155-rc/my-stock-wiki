# AAPL Q1 FY2026 Earnings Report
**Date:** December 27, 2025 (Period End) / January 29, 2026 (Release)
**Ticker:** [[AAPL]]

## Executive Summary
Apple reported its best-ever quarter with $143.8 billion in revenue, driven by staggering demand for the iPhone 17 lineup and a major recovery in Greater China (+38%).

## Financial Performance (Actuals)
| Metric | Q1 2026 (Act) | Q1 2025 (Act) | Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Net Sales** | $143,756M | $124,300M | +16% |
| **Net Income** | $42,097M | $36,330M | +16% |
| **Diluted EPS** | $2.84 | $2.40 | +19% |
| **Gross Margin %** | 48.2% | 45.9% | +230bps |

## Segment Net Sales
| Segment | Q1 2026 Sales | Q1 2025 Sales | Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **iPhone** | $85,269M | $69,138M | +23% |
| **Mac** | $8,386M | $8,987M | -7% |
| **iPad** | $8,595M | $8,088M | +6% |
| **Wearables, Home & Acc.** | $11,493M | $11,747M | -2% |
| **Services** | $30,013M | $26,340M | +14% |

## Guidance (Q2 FY2026)
- **Revenue Growth:** 13% - 16% YoY.
- **Gross Margin:** 48.0% - 49.0%.
- **Services:** Growth similar to Q1 (~14%).
- **Note:** Supply constraints on iPhone due to "advanced node capacity" at foundry partners.

## Ecosystem & News
- **[[TSM]]**: Explicitly cited as the bottleneck for iPhone supply due to advanced node capacity.
- **[[GOOGL]]**: Announced collaboration for next-generation Apple Foundation Models (AFM) to power Siri and Apple Intelligence.
- **Greater China:** Massive turnaround with $25.5B revenue (+38% YoY), driven by iPhone 17 Pro/Max.
- **Memory Costs:** CFO noted increasing market pricing for memory (NAND/DRAM) as a potential headwind for future margins.
