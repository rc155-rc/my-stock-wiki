# ASML Q1 FY2026 Earnings Report
**Date:** April 15, 2026
**Ticker:** [[ASML]]

## Executive Summary
ASML reported solid Q1 2026 results with net sales of €8.8 billion and a gross margin of 53.0%. The company raised its full-year 2026 outlook, citing that demand for AI-related chips is significantly outpacing supply.

## Financial Performance (Actuals)
| Metric | Q1 2026 (Act) | Q4 2025 (Act) | Change (QoQ) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Net Sales** | €8,767M | €9,718M | -9.8% |
| **Gross Margin %** | 53.0% | 52.2% | +80bps |
| **Net Income** | €2,757M | €2,840M | -2.9% |
| **EPS (Basic; Euros)** | €7.15 | €7.35 | -2.7% |

## Guidance & Forward Outlook
- **Q2 2026 Net Sales:** €8.4B - €9.0B.
- **FY 2026 Net Sales:** Raised to **€36B - €40B** (significant growth expectation).
- **FY 2026 Gross Margin:** 51% - 53%.
- **CEO Quote:** "Demand for chips is outpacing supply... customers are accelerating their capacity expansion plans for 2026 and beyond."

## Ecosystem & News
- **AI Infrastructure:** Explicitly cited as the primary driver for short and medium-term demand.
- **Export Controls:** Guidance accommodates potential outcomes of ongoing discussions regarding export restrictions.
- **Dividends:** Intends to declare a total dividend of €7.50 for FY2025, a 17% increase.
