# [[ASML]] - ASML Holding Master Profile

## 1. Corporate Identity
**Business Overview:** The world's leading manufacturer of lithography systems for the semiconductor industry. ASML is the sole provider of EUV (Extreme Ultraviolet) lithography machines.
**Leadership:** Christophe Fouquet (CEO).
**The Moat:** Absolute monopoly on EUV technology, which is required to manufacture any chip smaller than 7nm. High-NA EUV is the next generation of this moat.

## 2. Business Segments
- **EUV Lithography:** The high-end segment for 7nm, 5nm, 3nm, and 2nm nodes.
- **DUV Lithography:** Deep Ultraviolet systems for older/mature nodes.
- **Service & Options:** High-margin maintenance and software for the installed base.

## 3. The Ecosystem Graph
- **Suppliers:** [[ZEISS]], [[TRUMPF]], and specialized component manufacturers.
- **Customers:** [[TSM]], [[INTC]], [[SSNLF]], [[MU]], [[SK Hynix]].
- **Competitors:** [[Nikon]], [[Canon]] (only in DUV/legacy nodes).
