# GOOGL Q4 and FY 2025 Earnings Report
**Date:** February 4, 2026 (Release Date)
**Ticker:** [[GOOGL]]

## Executive Summary
Alphabet reported record revenues of $113.8B for Q4 2025 and surpassed $400B in annual revenue for the first time. Growth was driven by momentum in Google Search and a significant acceleration in Google Cloud (+48%).

## Financial Performance (Actuals)
| Metric | Q4 2025 (Act) | Q4 2024 (Act) | Change | FY 2025 (Act) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Revenue** | $113,828M | $96,469M | +18% | $402,836M |
| **Net Income** | $34,455M | $26,536M | +30% | $132,170M |
| **Diluted EPS** | $2.82 | $2.15 | +31% | $10.81 |
| **Operating Margin** | 31.6% | 32.0% | -40bps | 32.0% |

## Segment Net Sales (Q4)
| Segment | Q4 2025 Sales | Q4 2024 Sales | Change |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Google Search & other** | $63,073M | $54,034M | +17% |
| **YouTube ads** | $11,383M | $10,473M | +9% |
| **Google Network** | $7,828M | $7,954M | -2% |
| **Google Subscriptions, etc.** | $13,578M | $11,633M | +17% |
| **Google Cloud** | $17,664M | $11,955M | +48% |

## Guidance & Forward Outlook
- **2026 CapEx:** Anticipated to be in the range of **$175B to $185B**, representing a massive increase to support AI infrastructure.
- **AI Focus:** Gemini models now processing over 10 billion tokens per minute via direct API. Gemini App has 750 million monthly active users.
- **Cloud:** Annual run rate now over $70 billion.

## Ecosystem & News
- **[[NVDA]] / [[AVGO]]**: The massive $175B-$185B CapEx guidance is a direct catalyst for AI hardware suppliers.
- **[[AAPL]]**: Collaboration on Apple Foundation Models (AFM) noted as a milestone for Gemini distribution.
- **Waymo**: Announced a $16.0 billion investment round in February 2026, primarily funded by Alphabet.
