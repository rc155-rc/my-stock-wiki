# [[NVDA]] - NVIDIA Corporation Master Profile

## 1. Corporate Identity
**Business Overview:** NVIDIA is the pioneer of accelerated computing and the dominant provider of AI infrastructure. It provides full-stack solutions including GPUs, CPUs (Grace), DPUs, networking (Mellanox/InfiniBand), and software (CUDA, AI Enterprise).
**Leadership:** Jensen Huang (Founder & CEO), Colette Kress (CFO).
**The Moat:** The "CUDA Moat" (7.5M+ developers), the Blackwell architecture (world's most powerful AI chip), and the transition from a chip company to a data-center-scale systems company.

## 2. Business Segments
- **Compute & Networking ($193.5B FY26):** Includes Data Center (AI, HPC), Networking, and Automotive.
- **Graphics ($22.5B FY26):** Includes Gaming (GeForce RTX) and Professional Visualization.

- **Competitors:** [[AMD]], [[INTC]], [[GOOGL]] (TPUs), [[AMZN]] (Trainium/Inferentia), [[MSFT]] (Maia).
