# NVDA Q4 FY2026 Earnings Report
**Date:** February 25, 2026
**Ticker:** [[NVDA]]

## Executive Summary
NVIDIA reported record Q4 revenue of $68.1 billion, up 73% year-over-year, driven by extraordinary demand for the Blackwell AI platform. Full-year FY2026 revenue reached a staggering $215.9 billion.

## Financial Performance (Actuals)
| Metric | Q4 FY2026 (Act) | Q4 FY2025 (Act) | Change | FY2026 (Full Year) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Revenue** | $68,127M | $39,331M | +73% | $215,938M |
| **Net Income (GAAP)** | $42,960M | $22,091M | +94% | $120,067M |
| **Diluted EPS (GAAP)** | $1.76 | $0.89 | +98% | $4.90 |
| **Gross Margin (GAAP)** | 75.0% | 73.0% | +200bps | 71.1% |

## Market Platform Revenue (Q4)
- **Data Center:** $62,314M (+75% YoY) - Driven by Blackwell ramp and networking.
- **Gaming:** $3,727M (+47% YoY) - Sequentially down 13% on holiday seasonality.
- **Professional Visualization:** $1,321M (+159% YoY).
- **Automotive:** $604M (+6% YoY).

## Guidance (Q1 FY2027)
- **Revenue:** $78.0B +/- 2%.
- **Gross Margin:** 74.9% (GAAP) / 75.0% (Non-GAAP).
- **OpEx:** ~$7.7B (GAAP).

## Ecosystem & News
- **[[TSM]]**: Supply constraints noted for Gaming in FY2027; likely primary foundry for Blackwell.
- **Blackwell Ramp:** Exceptional demand noted for GB200 and GB300 systems.
- **Hyperscalers:** Account for slightly over 50% of Data Center revenue.
- **Intel Investment:** GAAP other income included gains from a previously announced investment in [[INTC]] common stock.
- **China:** Outlook assumes no Data Center revenue from China due to export restrictions.
