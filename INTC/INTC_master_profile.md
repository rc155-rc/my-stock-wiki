# [[INTC]] - Intel Corporation Master Profile

## 1. Corporate Identity
**Business Overview:** Intel is a world leader in the design and manufacturing of semiconductors. It is pivoting towards a "Foundry" model, aiming to be a major provider of semiconductor manufacturing capacity globally.
**Leadership:** Lip-Bu Tan (CEO), David Zinsner (CFO).
**The Moat:** IDM 2.0 strategy, leading-edge process technology (Intel 18A), massive manufacturing footprint in the U.S. and Europe, and x86 architecture dominance in client and server markets.

## 2. Business Segments
- **Client Computing Group (CCG):** Revenue from PC processors and related components.
- **Data Center and AI (DCAI):** Server processors, GPUs, and AI accelerators.
- **Intel Foundry:** Manufacturing services for internal and external customers.
- **Altera:** (Deconsolidated 2025) FPGA and programmable solutions.

## 3. The Ecosystem Graph
- **Suppliers:** [[ASML]] (Lithography), [[AMAT]], [[LRCX]], [[TSM]] (for certain tiles/chips).
- **Customers:** [[DELL]], [[HPQ]], [[LENOVO]], [[MSFT]], [[AMZN]], [[GOOGL]].
- **Competitors:** [[AMD]], [[NVDA]], [[TSM]] (Foundry), [[SSNLF]].
