# INTC Q4 and FY 2025 Earnings Report
**Date:** January 22, 2026
**Ticker:** [[INTC]]

## Executive Summary
Intel reported Q4 2025 revenue of $13.7B, exceeding expectations despite a 4% YoY decline. Full-year 2025 revenue was flat at $52.9B. The company is focused on its "Intel 18A" process technology and ramping manufacturing capacity in the U.S.

## Ecosystem & News
- **Intel 18A:** Ramped to high-volume manufacturing in Arizona and Oregon. First products (Intel Core Ultra Series 3) unveiled.
- **Cisco:** Announced collaboration on an integrated platform for distributed AI workloads.
- **NVIDIA:** Completed the sale of $5.0 billion of Intel common stock to [[NVDA]].
