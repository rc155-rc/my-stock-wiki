# 2026-04-09_News_Google_Collaboration
**Date:** April 9, 2026
**Ticker:** [[INTC]]

## Summary
Intel and [[GOOGL]] announced a multiyear collaboration to advance AI and cloud infrastructure. Intel Xeon processors will continue to power Google Cloud infrastructure, and the two companies are expanding co-development of custom ASIC-based infrastructure processing units (IPUs).

## Key Catalyst
- Expansion of co-development for custom ASIC-based IPUs to offload networking, storage, and security functions from host CPUs.
- Reinforcement of the central role of CPUs and IPUs in modern, heterogeneous AI systems.

## Market Sentiment
Positive. The collaboration validates Intel's relevance in the AI era and secures a major hyperscaler partnership.

## Ecosystem Impact
- **[[GOOGL]]**: Google Cloud gains specialized hardware to improve utilization and performance of its global infrastructure.
- **Suppliers**: Indirect positive for equipment suppliers like [[ASML]] as Intel ramps 18A capacity for these workloads.
